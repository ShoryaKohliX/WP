Scholarships for Top International Universities

1: Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded based on academic achievements, talents, or other skills.

2: Need-Based Scholarships: Given to students who demonstrate financial need.

3: Sports Scholarships: For students who excel in sports and contribute to the university's athletic programs.

4: Research Scholarships: Targeted at students who show potential in research or have research proposals.

5: International Scholarships: Designed for students from outside the host country, often focusing on cultural exchange.

Notable Universities and Their Scholarships

1: Harvard University: Provides full-tuition scholarships to students from families earning less than $65,000 a year.

2: University of Oxford: Covers full tuition and provides a stipend for living expenses for international students.

3: Stanford University: Provides need-based financial aid that can cover up to 100% of tuition.

4: ETH Zurich: Offers a scholarship covering living and study costs, along with a mentoring program.

5: University of Cambridge: Fully funds graduate students from any country, covering all costs.
